Get a taste for u-he’s soft synths by checking out the free TyrellN6.  The interface is straightforward and it sounds great.  I’ve also been having a lot of fun with ACE (Any Cable Everywhere), which is more full-featured than TyrellN6 but still very accessible in terms of UI and cost.

Here’s a look at TyrellN6 in Bitwig Studio.

 

u-he has another free, and more experimental, synth called the Triple Cheese.  I haven’t tried it yet but it’s installed and ready to go.
