Following up on the Rad-Fi synth post, Unit Unlikely has a similar, but simpler (fewer components, though soldering is required) and less expensive (~$20) kit synth, called the lunchbox that looks pretty interesting.

The Rad-Fi is more full-featured and extensible but the lunchbox looks like a fun project and another good way to get familiar with electronic sound making.  See the lunchbox manual for more info.
