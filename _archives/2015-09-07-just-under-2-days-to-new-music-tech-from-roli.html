Just under two days to ROLI's big announcement.  In April they announced Equator, a sound engine designed specifically to take advantage of the multidimensional nature of the Seaboard.  Perhaps the announcement is about some new combo of Equator with a smaller version of the Seaboard. Also of note, Bitwig Studio now has native support for the Seaboard and Linnstrument, both of which fall into the nascent class of multidimensional controllers that allow multiple parameters to be controlled for each note via Multidimensional Polyphonic Expression.

In any event, whatever the announcement is, it's sure to be interesting.  You can be one of the first to hear about what they're up to by signing up for their announcement mailer at roli.com.
